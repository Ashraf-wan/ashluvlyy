About Seol Hee

Seol Hee, known globally as K-Pop sensation Luna Snow, balances her life as a performer with her duties as a superhero. After being exposed to advanced cold fusion energy, she gained incredible cryokinetic powers, allowing her to manipulate ice for both stunning performances and formidable defense.

In Marvel Rivals, Luna Snow brings her unique blend of agility, crowd control, and icy attacks to the battlefield. Explore her abilities and stunning design from the game below!
